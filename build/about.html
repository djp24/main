Hi, I'm Dara

I have had an interest in web development & design since I was a kid, and the first sites I ever made were anime fansites using Freewebs in 2005. However, I didn't start to seriously study up on various coding languages until 2021. This site is my first serious project since then that isn't just a school project or a copy of a tutorial. It also uses Tailwind CSS.

On my off-time, I enjoy drawing, gaming, and watching shows I've already watched. I also dabble in translating fancomics from Japanese -> English, but I'm still studying. When I'm both busy and not busy, I also enjoy listening to music-- I wouldn't say I listen to anything, but my listening interests are vast.

Some of my favorite places of all time include Himeji and Tokyo. I have massive wanderlust, so one day I'd love to travel to other places in Japan and the world!
